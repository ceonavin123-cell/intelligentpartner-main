# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## 1. What We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for login)
- Name (if provided)

### Usage Data
- Chat messages with the AI consulting platform
- Uploaded documents (PDFs, text files)
- Company profiles you create

### Technical Data
- IP address (for rate limiting and security)
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited on the platform

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service**: Process your documents, run AI analysis, generate reports
- **Improve the platform**: Analyze usage patterns to improve AI accuracy
- **Security**: Rate limiting, fraud detection, audit logging
- **Compliance**: Legal obligations if required

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in **Supabase** (PostgreSQL database)
- Data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL)
- We use **Row Level Security** — you can only access your own data
- API keys are stored server-side only (never exposed to browsers)
- Regular database backups are maintained

## 4. AI & Data Processing

- Your documents are processed by **Google Gemini** (for PDF extraction and embeddings)
- Your chat messages are processed by **NVIDIA NIM** (for AI responses)
- Your data is **NOT** used to train AI models
- AI responses may contain inaccuracies — always verify important information

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or share your data with third parties except:
- **AI providers** (Google Gemini, NVIDIA NIM) — only for processing your requests
- **法律要求** — if required by law or court order

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

- **Access**: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Request your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to data processing for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, contact: **privacy@intelligentpartner.ai**

## 7. Data Retention

- **Chat messages**: Deleted after 90 days
- **Documents**: Retained while your account is active
- **Memories**: Decay over time (60 days of inactivity)
- **Reports**: Retained for 1 year

## 8. Cookies

- **Necessary cookies**: Required for authentication and security
- **Analytics cookies**: Optional — help us improve the platform
- **Marketing cookies**: Optional — personalized content

You can manage cookie preferences via the consent banner on first visit.

## 9. Children's Privacy

This platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
